# Privacy Checklist

Before publishing or sharing:

- Remove real company names.
- Remove real tax IDs.
- Remove real representatives.
- Remove real phone numbers.
- Remove real addresses.
- Remove real site names.
- Remove customer-specific remarks that reveal identity.
- Use obviously fake sample data such as `範例汽車服務股份有限公司`, `00000000`, `王小明`, `02-0000-0000`, and `臺北市範例區測試路100號`.
- Search the final folder for known customer keywords before uploading.

Do not upload insurer replies, customer quote PDFs, marked-up PDFs, or policy documents unless the user explicitly confirms they are anonymized or safe to publish.
